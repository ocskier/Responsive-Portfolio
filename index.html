About Me

Hello! I was born in Louisiana and grew up in Richmond, Va until graduating from high school. I attended college at NC State University and graduated with dual B.S. degrees in Chemical Engineering and Wood and Paper Science. I then attended graduate school where I obtained a Computer Programming Certificate and a Masters in Wood and Paper Science.

My family life and personal interests have led me to spend the most recent years teaching myself hardware and software fundamentals utilizing freelance work. I spent sometime learning coding as well as building, repairing, and implementing personal computers.

I am looking to gain full-time employment in software development at this juncture using skills learned up to and including the UNC Chapel Hill Coding Bootcamp.
